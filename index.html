How the InfoVerify Engine Works

        
A simple 3-step process combining Data Extraction and AI Text Analysis.

        
        

            

                

                
1. Reading the Article

                
The system safely visits the link and extracts just the main text, leaving behind all the messy ads and pop-ups.

            

            

                

                
2. Checking for Bias

                
Our AI reads the text to see if the author is using emotional, sensational, or clickbait words to manipulate the reader.

            

            

                

                
3. Verifying the Source

                
We check the website against a global database to see if it's a trusted news site, a known fake news network, or unsecure.
